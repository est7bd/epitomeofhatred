Jack Smith Memo: Trump Kept Classified Docs for His Business.

An internal memo from the former special counsel's office says Trump retained classified government documents relevant to his business interests after leaving office. It was transmitted to Congress. It's not a rumor. It's a government document.

← all posts

Remember when Trump said he kept boxes of classified documents at Mar-a-Lago because he had a right to them? Remember when his supporters said it was a witch hunt, a hoax, a politically motivated attack? An internal memo from former special counsel Jack Smith's office tells a different story.

What the Memo Says

Trump retained government documents relevant to his business interests after he left office. The memo was transmitted by the Justice Department to the House and Senate Judiciary Committees earlier this month. Source: POLITICO

Let's Be Precise About What This Means.

This isn't a political operative saying Trump did something wrong. This is an internal memo from the office of the special counsel appointed to investigate this very question — transmitted through official DOJ channels to Congress. It says Trump kept classified government documents because they were useful for his personal business.

Not because he thought he had a legal right to them. Not because of some paperwork mix-up. Because they were useful to him financially.

He took classified government documents. He kept them. The special counsel's office concluded it was to advance his business interests. This was transmitted to Congress as an official finding.

And Nothing Will Happen.

The same DOJ that transmitted this memo is now run by Trump's own appointees. The same Congress that received it is controlled by people who spent two years calling this investigation a hoax. Trump has already fired the prosecutors who built the case, pardoned himself of federal charges, and installed loyalists throughout the Justice Department.

A special counsel's memo says he took classified documents for business purposes. It was sent to Congress. And it will be filed away and forgotten, because the people who would normally act on it are the same people who benefit from doing nothing.

This is where we are.
