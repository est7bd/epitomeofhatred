Marie Yovanovitch was a career ambassador. She was also a problem for Trump-world because she represented the boring old idea that U.S. diplomacy is supposed to serve national interests instead of the president's domestic political dirt-hunting operation. So they smeared her and got her removed.

Why she was targeted

Witnesses and impeachment evidence showed that Yovanovitch stood in the way of the pressure campaign aimed at getting Ukraine to announce investigations helpful to Trump politically.

The attack on her was not random. It was a warning shot to every professional in the foreign-policy system: if you do not play along, the circus will target you and the White House will not protect you.

Corruption Needed Clearance Space.

That is what Yovanovitch's removal did. It cleared space. Once serious people were pushed aside, the shadow channel could do its work through Rudy Giuliani, loyalists, and conspiracy-fed nonsense.

Trump did not just abuse Ukraine. He also degraded the institutions that might have resisted the abuse.
