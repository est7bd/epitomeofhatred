How this page works

What counts

Entries belong here when the story centers on private benefit, public abuse, donor influence, conflicts of interest, weaponized pardons, or state power bent toward personal gain.

How it is framed

Each entry breaks the scheme into four parts: what happened, who benefited, what the public paid, and why it matters. Clean structure beats outrage sludge.

Every card sends readers into your existing reporting, so this page becomes a hub instead of a dead end. Receipts first. Rant second.

How to expand it

Add new cards as new schemes break, then split major stories into dedicated explainers when a single corruption thread gets too big to fit on one card.
